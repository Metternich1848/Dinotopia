The Mysterious Mesozoic: The Age of Reptiles!

Deriving from the Greek words " meso" and "zoon", the term "Mesozoic" refers to a geological era that roughly extended from about 252 million years ago to around 66 million years ago. This time span is divided into three major dinosaur periods, the Triassic, Jurassic and Cretaceous ages. Since this epoch was the peak of the reptile-like animals, it was also known as the “Age of Reptiles”.

Amber

The Most Beautiful Fossil

Amber is a kind of hard fossilized tree resin that was formed millions of years ago. Sometimes small insects like ants or mosquitoes were found trapped inside a resin. This type of natural stone comes in different colors, ranging from orange yellow to reddish brown. Today, amber is made into different forms of luxury jewelry products, from earrings to necklaces.
